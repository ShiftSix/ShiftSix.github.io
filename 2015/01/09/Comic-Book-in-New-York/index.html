Comic Book in New York

By Lian.Liu

Jan 9 2015 Updated:Jan 19 2015

Contents

In many ways, New York City is quite like Shanghai. The skyscrapers drop your hat when you try to count the floors; the crowd pushes you to move no matter you want or not; the luxury brands shine in windows innocently towards tourists from all around the world; the traffic is always terrible in downtown area; people with different backgrounds and religions gather in their own group but get along with each others as well.
I do enjoy the clear blue sky here, which is becoming more and more rare in Shanghai these years. If ignoring some of the old, sticky, creepy subway stations, this should be a nice city to live in.
Anyway, I properly used my imaginations to the pictures taken in New York City. Or there would be no fun at all to show those monotonous METROPOLISES images.
