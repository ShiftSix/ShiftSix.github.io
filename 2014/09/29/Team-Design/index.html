This is an optional assignment from our Project Management class. The requirements are as follows.

====================================
You are professional consultants offering Project Management related services!
You are developing a means to communicate Who you are and What you offer!
Here are the things we are looking for:

  • Best Team Name
  • Best Team Logo
  • Best Team Motto

AuroReal

Team Members

Team 7: Akash Gupta, Aaron Shakin, Lian Liu, Mohammad Ali Raahim

About the Team

AuroReal is a project management consulting team focused on commercializing programs. We provide start-up, emerging and well-established companies with useful insights for improving their commercialization decisions.
Commercialization is one of the most significant stages of the technological innovation process due to its high risks and costs. At AuroReal, we work as advisors as well as your collaborators during this crucial stage to help you avoid errors that could translate into serious market failure.
AuroReal is made up of four professional consultants with expertise in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Product Development, Automation and Control Systems. The team has comprehensive experience in technical marketing, digital marketing and intellectual property. We are here to support you taking decisive actions and achieve sustainable results.

About the Name

AuroReal comes from Aurora and Realization. The goal of the team is to help companies realize emerging high-tech innovations, as the aurora of our time, into real market shares.

Team Logo


AuroReal helps move your business from the Red Ocean to the Blue Ocean and ensures sustainable growth by providing commercialization consulting services.

Team Mascot


The Blue Ocean recalls everyone of this smart animal, dolphin. AuroReal Dolphin represents the team’s knowledge, wisdom and energetic spirit. The smooth design can be easily and clearly applied to different kinds of materials in addition to printing, such as T-shirts, trophies, packages and company giveaways. With no additional accessories AuroReal Dolphin can be designed into 3D samples and the wide back of the dolphin leaves adequate space to print slogans or QR code to deliver more information.

Team Motto

We help you realize your technology and win the market as a team.

Designing Reference:

CG3, Deloitte, Erie Innovation and Commercialization, Caterpillar

About the Team

All four team members of AuroReal are students from Duke MEMP (Master of Engineering Management Program). The program prepares engineering and science graduates to become future industry leaders.

I felt guilty not winning the 100 dollars prize for our team with this design. In general, the concept was comprehensive and creative. But the artwork was a little crude. I wanted to say at that time, I had just been in the US for less than two months. I was content with my achievement of making up all these instructions in English from nothing. But this is life, no excuse. I have to do better next time.
